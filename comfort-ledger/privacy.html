Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Comfort Ledger collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account/session data needed to keep you logged in.
  • Finance inputs you provide (income, expenses, debts, recurring payments, goals).
  • Technical metadata required for operation and security (for example, basic request logs).

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core product features.
  • Maintain service reliability and security.
  • Process subscriptions and billing status.

3. Payments

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy (Merchant of Record). We do not store full credit card data on our servers.

4. AI Features

If AI features are enabled, selected prompt context may be sent to OpenAI to generate responses. Do not submit sensitive credentials or card numbers in free-text prompts.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account data where applicable.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with subprocessors necessary to operate the service (e.g., payment and AI providers).

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this page.

10. Contact
